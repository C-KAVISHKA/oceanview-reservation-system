Creating a New Reservation

Step-by-step booking process

1
Click "New Booking" from the navigation bar
2
Guest Information
  • Full Name (required, 2-100 chars)
  • Email Address (required, valid format)
  • Contact Number (required, e.g. +94-77-123-4567)
  • Address (required)
3
Reservation Details
  • Check-In Date (today or future)
  • Check-Out Date (after check-in)
  • Room Type — select from visual room cards
  • Number of Guests (1-10)
  • Special Requests (optional, max 500 chars)
4
Click "Create Reservation" — you'll see an estimated cost preview before submitting
5
The system displays the Reservation ID upon success

Viewing & Managing Reservations

Search, filter, and manage all bookings

1
Click "Reservations" from the navigation bar
2
Use the search bar to filter by guest name, email, or reservation ID
3
Use filter tabs (All / Pending / Confirmed / Cancelled) to narrow results
4
Click "View" for full details or "Delete" to cancel (requires confirmation)

Status Reference

PENDING Reservation created, awaiting confirmation
CONFIRMED Reservation confirmed and active
CANCELLED Reservation cancelled

Generating Bills

Invoice generation and printing

1
Click "Billing" from the navigation bar
2
Enter the Reservation ID (e.g. 1, 2, 3...)
3
Click "Generate Bill" to create the invoice
4
Review the invoice with guest info, charges breakdown, service charge (5%), tax (8%), and grand total
5
Click "Print Invoice" to print or save as PDF
