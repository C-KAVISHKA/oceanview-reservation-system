Help & Documentation

User guide for the OceanView Reservation System

Getting Started

Welcome to the OceanView Reservation System! This guide will help you navigate and use all the features.

Login

Use your staff credentials to log in. Demo credentials:

  • Email: staff@oceanview.com
  • Password: Passw0rd!

Creating a New Reservation

To create a new reservation:

  1. Click "New Booking" from the navigation bar
  2. Fill in the guest information:
    • Full Name (required, 2-100 characters)
    • Email Address (required, valid email format)
    • Contact Number (required, format: +1-555-0101)
    • Address (required)
  3. Fill in reservation details:
    • Check-In Date (must be today or future date)
    • Check-Out Date (must be after check-in)
    • Room Type (Single LKR 30,000, Double LKR 45,000, Suite LKR 75,000, Deluxe LKR 120,000)
    • Number of Guests (1-10)
    • Special Requests (optional, max 500 characters)
  4. Click "Create Reservation"
  5. The system will display the reservation ID upon success

Viewing & Managing Reservations

To view all reservations:

  1. Click "Reservations" from the navigation bar
  2. Use the search bar to filter by guest name, email, or reservation ID
  3. Click "View" to see full reservation details
  4. Click "Delete" to cancel a reservation (requires confirmation)

Reservation Status

  • PENDING — Reservation created, awaiting confirmation
  • CONFIRMED — Reservation confirmed
  • CANCELLED — Reservation cancelled

Generating Bills

To generate a bill for a reservation:

  1. Click "Billing" from the navigation bar
  2. Enter the Reservation ID
  3. Click "Generate Bill"
  4. The system will display guest info, reservation details, room charges, service charge (5%), tax (8%), and grand total
  5. Click "Print Bill" to print or save as PDF

Tip: You can also click "View Bill" from the reservation details modal on the Reservations page.

Reports & Analytics

Occupancy Report

View room occupancy for a specific date:

  1. Click "Reports" from the navigation bar
  2. Select a date and click "Generate Report"
  3. View total rooms, occupied, available, occupancy rate, and breakdown by room type

Revenue Report

View revenue for a date range:

  1. Select From Date and To Date
  2. Click "Generate Report"
  3. View total revenue, total reservations, average booking value, revenue by room type, and daily breakdown

Room Types & Rates

Billing Calculations

  • Room Charges: Rate per night × Number of nights
  • Service Charge: 5% of room charges
  • Tax: 8% of room charges
  • Grand Total: Room charges + Service charge + Tax
